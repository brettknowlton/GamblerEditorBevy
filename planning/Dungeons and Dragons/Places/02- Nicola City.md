Nicola City is a large civilization mostly inhabited by humans. They were quite adept with their technology and progressed quite far beyond the timeline most thought possible. They were the last of the [[5 Major Encampments]] to fall to the rot, most of the surviving members were lost in the trek.

Constituents of Nicola City are generally racist against non-humans, they became racially ubiquitous as time progressed in the rot. Some changelings were able to set up comfortably there but other races were largely shunned to disperse before the rot fell upon the city.

People to leave early to the Grand West are especially shunned by those from Nicola City- called "Godless" people as most who travel to these lands forget or abandon their dieties.
