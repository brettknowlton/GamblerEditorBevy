The front gate of camp ernest is a large draw-bridge like gate that opens up to a vast plain outside. 50+ Miles out to the East another mountain range can be seen placing Camp Ernest on the entirely westward side of a canyon.

You can see both the far off range as well as the closely nested mountain that camp ernest tucks iteslf into.
A farmers hut sits on the outer corner near the path outwards from the gate on one side, and the other side clumps mass ammounts of tents from refugees seeking home at the camp

the military has temporarily set up a long tent to house extra soldiers and process people quicker as there is a quite large influx of new people to on-board and get understanding.


A large cascade of wheat fields lay out along the massive walls of camp Ernest, 2 guard towers are occupied by avian types keeping a watchful eye on the surroundings

Following the path out about a mile it forks with 1 trail heading south west around the mountain and other to the north east where most newcomers are received from.
