Melborne was a servant of [[15- The Devil|Sir Ashborne]], the owner of the [[Ashborne Manor]]

He is a lost boy at heart, homeless most his life he does not really value relationships with people the same most do

He values a work relationship that is very direct. He does not grasp money all that well.
He is a 1/4 Tiefling with 1 Demonic Grandparent making him 1/8th Demon.

He has Grey Eyes and Hair and ominously pale skin. He fits in well with the other humans from [[02- Nicola City]] and they recognize him as having most of the same customs.


He does not know [[Endrin (Riley)]] even though they were physically close, Niccola City is massive.
