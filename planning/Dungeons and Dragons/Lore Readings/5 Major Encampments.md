Before the 5 Encampments there was a grand nation. Split across the continent 22 countries came together to form the grand counsel. Underlings, Half-Breeds, and PureBloods alike each used their own system of election to grant one member of their Nation the title of Apostle. The Apostles would meet at a building known only as "The Tower" to make decisions on a grand scale. No nation had say or control over another's resources or people, but this international congress mostly functioned cleanly.

The breakout of the rot would soon bring an end to this utopic government.

When the rot began mostly underground the Gnomes and Goblinfolk formed a union to pester other nations for their help in fighting it. After some time- different nations were less inclined to aid those of other living conditions and kind and one by one they disbanded their approval and commitments to the tower.

Around this time the first modern-day pioneers forged their way to the grand west. Goblins and Gnomes arrived first and it did not take long for them to locate the first of many magical irregularities that lied beneath that half of the earth. and then the rumors of insanity stared... beings of great power suddenly and mysteriously changing all their lives' purpose. People in power disappearing off the face of the earth- as if they themselves had forgotten they were supposed to be there.

The Tower's power crumbled and what remained was 5 proud nations that over-powered or overtook the others.

The rot was unforgiving though. After 5 years and the loss of nearly 80% of life, these nations each crumbled to shadows of what they were. 

Loss of infrastructure, manpower, economies each took their turn battering the life in the Rotten Continent. 

eventually nothing was left but the grand west, and those who went insane staying out of it anyway.
