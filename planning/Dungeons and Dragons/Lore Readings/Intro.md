Torat Camp - Year 15 of the Rot
Spring Equinox

We've been here for 3 years now. Despite the warm winter our messengers have still not returned. At this point we must assume the worst. The Grand west has been a harsh home but it may very well be the final bastion of intelligent life.

Scouts have seen early signs of the rot about 100 miles out. There may not be many options for us left.

The runes may have to be the answer. I know how my people feel about it but can it really be worse than what has already come to the rest of the world?

Maybe things would be better if we never would have interacted with them, but we did and now we know it just might work. The girl may be able to fix this if she can handle all four. Can she? I might not have a choice. We've lost so many already, and with no-one new on the way... I can only foresee the worst.

She might be the key to all of this though.

Finally a way to end this story. I promise you my brothers, our journey will not be wasted. We will create our paradise. You will find your peace.

//General A. W.
