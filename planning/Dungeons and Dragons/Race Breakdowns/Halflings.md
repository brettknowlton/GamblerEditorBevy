Halflings are the quiet souls who thrive in the shadows of greater powers, their small stature belying their boundless spirit. Content with simple joys—good food, warm hearths, and close-knit communities—they avoid the grand struggles of the world, preferring cleverness to brute force. Yet, when fate calls, halflings rise with uncanny *luck and resilience*, proving that true courage isn’t about size or strength, but about facing the impossible with a grin.
