Goblins are creatures of cunning and survival, their small frames hiding a boundless energy for chaos and invention. Once seen as nothing more than pests, they have adapted in ways few could imagine, turning scrap into weapons and ruins into fortresses. Quick-witted and unpredictable, goblins thrive on the edge of society, their mischievous nature hiding a fierce loyalty to those they call their own. What they lack in strength, they make up for in sheer audacity, always ready to seize whatever the world refuses to give them.
