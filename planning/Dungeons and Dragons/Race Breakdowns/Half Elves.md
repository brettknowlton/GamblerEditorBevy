Half-elves walk the line between two worlds, never quite belonging to either. Born of elven grace and human ambition, they embody the strengths and struggles of both, blessed with extended lifespans yet cursed with the knowledge that they will always be outsiders. Some embrace their duality, wandering between cultures, while others forge their own path, refusing to be defined by heritage alone. They are the bridge between fleeting passion and patient wisdom, forever searching for where they truly belong.
