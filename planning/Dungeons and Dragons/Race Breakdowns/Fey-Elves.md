Eladrin are elves forever bound to the shifting tides of the Feywild, their emotions and seasons intertwined. More fey than mortal, they are mercurial and unpredictable, changing with the flow of magic itself. Each season represents an aspect of their being—passionate summer, melancholic autumn, patient winter, and hopeful spring. They are wanderers of the veiled world, their presence both enchanting and unsettling, as if reality itself bends around their will.
