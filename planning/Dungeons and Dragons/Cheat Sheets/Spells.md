
## **Spell Components**
These describe what is required to **cast** the spell.

1. **Verbal (V)**
    
    - You must be able to speak.
        
    - Can't cast if silenced or gagged.
        
2. **Somatic (S)**
    
    - You need a free hand to perform gestures.
        
    - Can’t cast if restrained or hands are full.
        
3. **Material (M)**
    
    - Requires specific items (e.g., a small crystal, a pinch of sulfur).
        
    - **You don’t need to track materials if you have a component pouch or arcane focus**, unless the spell lists a cost (e.g., "a diamond worth 300 gp").

### . **Spell Classes**

Spells are limited by **which classes can cast them**.  
Common spellcasting classes:

- **Full Casters**: Wizard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Bard, Warlock
    
- **Half Casters**: Paladin, Ranger
    
- **Third Casters** (via subclass or feat): Eldritch Knight, Arcane Trickster, Artificer



|School|Focus|
|---|---|
|**Abjuration**|Protection, wards, banishment|
|**Conjuration**|Summoning, teleportation|
|**Divination**|Information, foresight|
|**Enchantment**|Mind control, charm|
|**Evocation**|Direct energy (fireballs, lightning)|
|**Illusion**|Deception, disguises|
|**Necromancy**|Life/death magic, undead|
|**Transmutation**|Altering matter or form|




### **Casting Time**

- Most are **1 action**
    
- Others: **Bonus action**, **Reaction**, **1 minute**, **10 minutes**, **1 hour**, etc.


### **Range & Area**

- **Touch**, **Self**, or specific distances (30 ft, 120 ft, etc.)
    
- Area types:
    
    - Line
        
    - Cone
        
    - Cube
        
    - Sphere
        
    - Cylinder


### **Duration**

- **Instantaneous**
    
- **Concentration** (maintain with focus, broken if damaged or casting another concentration spell)
    
- Set durations (e.g., 1 minute, 1 hour, 24 hours)


### **Target**

- A creature, object, area, or self.
    
- Can include multiple targets at higher levels.


### **Saving Throw / Attack Roll**

- Some spells require the target to **make a saving throw** (Dex, Wis, Con, etc.).
    
- Others require a **spell attack roll** from the caster.
    
- Some have **no save or attack**, just happen (e.g., _Misty Step_).



### **Scaling**

- Many spells **scale when cast at higher levels**, e.g., _Cure Wounds_, _Fireball_, _Spirit Guardians_.
