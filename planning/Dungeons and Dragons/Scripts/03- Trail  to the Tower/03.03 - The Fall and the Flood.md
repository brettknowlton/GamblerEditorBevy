## ⚔️ **ACT 3: “The Fall and the Flood”**

> _The path winds into the canyon’s mouth like a throat of stone. Steep walls rise on either side, and the air feels damp — as though it rained here recently. But above you, only clouds whisper between sharp peaks. The Geos snort and stop. Then… they move again, unbidden._

---

### 🪨 **Scene 1: The Canyon's Mouth**

**Description**:

- The trail narrows abruptly. The wagon scrapes stone.
    
- Sheer drop to the right — hundreds of feet into a misty gorge.
    
- Small broken rails once guarded the edge, now splinters.
    
- Vegetation is slick, mossy. A subtle trembling hum begins as the Geos move.
    

---

### 🐂 **Geo Disobedience Begins**

> Without a word, the Geos _begin moving forward again_. But something’s changed — they no longer heed the reins, nor the driver’s voice. Their heads are tilted slightly to the sky, as if _listening to something far away._

**Allow reactions**:

- Vesta may try **Animal Handling** (DC 14) — fails or partial success results in a brief pause but not control.
    
- GibGab may offer weird tinkering logic — have fun with it (“Maybe they’re magnetized!”).
    
- Lucas might sense nature _resisting_ human instruction.
    

> “It’s not them. It’s something calling them.”

**Then...** a player on the cliffside is pushed.  
Let it be the one most dramatically positioned. You can choose based on RP, but if unsure — GibGab or Melborne.

---

### 🐕‍🦺 **Scene 2: The Fall**

> A wheel strikes a loose rock. The Geo lurches sideways.  
> [Player], walking beside it, stumbles and crashes against the canyon wall — loose gravel gives way beneath them.  
> _Melborne’s pack flips forward — the pup wriggles out mid-yelp, claws at the edge… then slips._

**Slow motion description**:

- The pup’s white fur catches sunlight.
    
- Its little claws grasp at air.
    
- A moment of perfect silence.
    

> _Melborne reaches, leaping from the cart._  
> Someone grabs his arm — Lucas? Rolen? Vesta?  
> He looks them in the eye — and lets go.

**End the scene here mid-action if dramatic. Or narrate...**

> He disappears over the cliff edge.

Let the party rush to the edge.  
They see:

- A flash of white far below.
    
- **No sign of Melborne.**
    
- Then... the earth rumbles.
    

---

### 🌊 **Scene 3: The Flood (Conditional)**

If the players **did not tithe** at the altar:

> A distant roar builds. Water begins pouring from cracks high in the canyon walls. A **flash flood** begins crashing down the path from behind.

- They must either **run**, **jump the cart onto high ground**, or **brace for impact.**
    

**Skill Challenge (DC 12s)**:

- **Strength** to push the cart.
    
- **Dexterity** to climb or grab ropes.
    
- **Wisdom** to spot escape paths.
    
- **Constitution** to hold breath if submerged.
    

> **Failure** costs HP or leaves a character clinging to a wet ledge. **Success** means they reach a high, natural ledge in time — soaked and shaken.

If they **did tithe**, the water _builds_, then diverts down a split in the canyon just before reaching them.

> “The water seemed to pause — then retreat. As if the mountain itself was protecting you.”

---

### 🌀 **Scene 4: The Final Pull**

As they catch their breath, the Geos — still on the ledge — begin slowly walking again.  
This time, without orders.  
They look **uphill**, toward where the canyon wraps left toward The Tower.

**Insight check (DC 13)**:

> “They aren’t afraid. They’re answering a call.”

The air becomes **still and crystalline**, the rock walls shimmer faintly with light — as if **Helena Shinstar** were watching.

Let the moment breathe.

---

### 🎬 **Session Ending**

**Closing Scene**:

> The cart creaks as it moves again. The sun begins to set, blood-orange through the clouds. Somewhere far above, a tower glows faintly — its peak just visible in the mist.

> The group is smaller now.

Fade out.
