**Combat: Corrupted Dire Bear Cub** (homebrew Level 1 mini-boss)

- Think of it as a **teenager bear**, wounded, driven mad by **the Rot**.
    
- Stats based on **Brown Bear**, reduced slightly for balance:
    
    - **HP**: 35
        
    - **AC**: 11
        
    - **Multiattack**: 1 bite, 1 claw
        
    - **Rot Burst (1/Day)**: When reduced below 10 HP, explodes spores in a 10-ft radius (Con save DC 12 or take 1d6 poison + Disadv. on next attack/save)
        

**Tactical Behavior**:

- Goes after anyone carrying meat or smelling like animals (Melborne, Lucas, possibly Geos).
    
- If GibGab casts anything sparkly or noisy → becomes target.
    
- If Vesta heals, she may be seen as a “threat to the sickness” and attacked.
    

**Make it intense**: This is the moment the group feels real pain — maybe someone goes down to 0 HP and is revived.
