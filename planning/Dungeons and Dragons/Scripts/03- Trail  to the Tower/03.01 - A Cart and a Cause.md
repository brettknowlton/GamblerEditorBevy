### 🏞️ **Scene Setup**

- Morning. The party finishes final prep at the base of the valley.
    
- Wagon is packed. Geos huff restlessly, awaiting command.
    
- The sun is filtered by thin cloud cover; the valley floor is green but quiet.
    

---

### 🚶‍♂️PARTY CONFIGURATION

**Who’s driving?** Let players choose. Good options:

- Vesta (calm with animals),
    
- Rolen (experienced with travel),
    
- GibGab (claims he's got “engineer instincts”),
    
- or they hand it off to a hired NPC if they’re unsure.
    

**Where is everyone?**

- Prompt: “Tell me where your character is riding or walking. On the cart? Leading the geos? Scouting ahead?”
    

**📦 Michael Scandopa (REQUIRED Interaction 0)**

- **Trigger**: After ~10 minutes of travel.
    
- GibGab hears a strange _snore-sneeze_ sound from under a blanket near the back.
    
- Michael pops out with:
    
    > “Don’t throw me off, alright? Camp Ernest didn’t sit right. That’s all. Felt it in my gut.”
    

Let players question him. He will deflect with humor or half-truths:

- “Aren’t you glad I’m here? I know at least three sea shanties and one of them’s about mountains.”
    

📌 **Contingency Options**:

- If they **kick him out**: He walks behind silently, which may prompt Vesta or Melborne to offer him a ride later.
    
- If they **interrogate him**: He won’t reveal visions, but may slip in something odd like:
    
    > “I just… keep seeing waves. And fire. But I’m not a prophet or anything weird like that.”
    

---

### 🐺 **REQUIRED: Lucas Attack (Interaction 1)**

**Trigger**: Late morning, still in valley before the canyon.

**Setup**:

- Let the group roll Perception (DC 13) — those who pass sense movement in the woods.
    
- _Arrow lands near Rolen’s feet._
    
- Lucas yells from nearby brush:
    
    > “Let. Him. Go.”
    

**Mechanics**:

- Roll **Animal Handling** (Lucas): `d20 ÷ 5`, rounded up = number of wolves.
    
- Wolves surround or push toward the cart.
    

**Lucas believes** Rolen is being manipulated or taken. He's scared, emotional, not murderous.

- “You don't understand! I saw what the Rot does. And you—” (points to GibGab or Verity) “—you’re strangers dragging him off!”
    

📌 **Contingency Options**:

- **Combat**: Wolves only attack if attacked first. One may sniff the pup if the cart is searched.
    
- **De-escalation**: Vesta can calm him, GibGab can distract him (“I’m a wizard!”), Rolen can talk him down.
    
- Lucas will join if calmed, walking or riding reluctantly beside the cart.
    

---

### 🐾 **Melborne’s Dog Reveal (Interaction 2)**

**Trigger**: During or after Lucas confrontation.

- One wolf gets curious about the bag and whimpers.
    
- Michael might say, “Hey, your bag’s leaking growls.”
    
- Pup pops out, adorable and _clearly_ not ordinary.
    

**Reaction**:

- Lucas is stunned. “You’ve been carrying a wolf?”
    
- Pup runs in circles, maybe nips Lucas’s hand affectionately.
    

📌 **Contingency Options**:

- If players try to **hide the pup**, it howls at a key moment.
    
- If they **embrace the reveal**, use the pup as a recurring empathy point — especially later.
    

---

### 🌧️ **Optional Threads (Prep for Act 2)**

These can be layered into travel or set up at the **entrance to the canyon**:

- **NPC travelers on the road**, including someone sick on a stretcher.
    
    - Melborne or Vesta may notice advanced rot signs.
        
    - If helped, the group gains info: “The canyon floods sometimes. There’s a shrine somewhere that can stop it.”
        
- **The Altar** is visible on a cliffside path. Party may bypass it unless someone makes a high enough Perception/Insight check or reads Celestial.
    
    - Vesta or Verity might understand it directly.
