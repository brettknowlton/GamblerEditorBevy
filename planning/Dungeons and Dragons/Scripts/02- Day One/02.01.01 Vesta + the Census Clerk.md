***Uses Reference Sheet:*** [[Camp Ernest – Census Interview Log]]
### 🎧 **Census Clerk – Larren Kelt**

**Voice**: Nasal, flat, fast-talking—like someone who’s been talking all day and wishes he wasn’t.  
**Personality**: Bureaucratic, passive-aggressive, clearly doesn’t want to be there, but knows his job.  
**Appearance**: Stringy gray-blond hair tied in a frizzy bun, ink-stained fingertips, wearing a coat two sizes too big.

> “[[Vesta Wonderfoot(Emma)]], right? You’re the one with the big opinions. General said you’re _helpful._ You’re up.”  
> _(Waves a handful of forms in your direction)_  
> “Take these. You know how to read, right? Good. Interview the front bench. Start with the one who looks like she just wandered out of a dream.”  
> _(Mutters as you leave)_  
> “If she bites you, let the medic know.”

**How to Guide the Scene**:  
Tell Vesta’s player:

> _“You’re inside a stuffy canvas tent that smells like old ink and sweat. A bored-looking clerk half-buries you in census forms and points you toward a mismatched row of sitting refugees. One woman’s wearing mismatched boots. One man is wrapped in two scarves. Another looks like he hasn’t blinked in an hour. The first person on your list is staring at a wooden beam like it’s giving her orders.”_

- Vesta is working off someone else’s orders—**passed down from General Ernest**—to **interview and record new arrivals**. She’s seen as reliable, if a little annoying.
    
- The officer running the tent says: _“Cleric, take this list and fill it out. You’ve been here long enough to know what counts as a lie.”_
