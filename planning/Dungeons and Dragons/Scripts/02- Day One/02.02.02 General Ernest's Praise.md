### 🎬 _Scene 2: General Ernest Appears_

- The golems are defeated, the field **trashed**, and people are panicked.
    
- From the barracks, **General Ernest storms in**, visibly furious but restrained.
    
- He doesn’t yell. He studies the players.
    

> **“I’m told you handled yourselves. Protected people.”**  
> **“I’ve got bigger problems than who filed what form. Gemlings are acting up near the mountain passes.”**  
> **“You’ll take this to Camp Fairwick. Travel light. You leave at dawn.”**

- The group is stunned—**they’re nobodies.** Why them?
    

> “Because you reacted. Most people ran. You didn’t.”  
> “That’s enough, for now.”

> He **does not assign GibGab.** In fact, he doesn't mention him at all.
