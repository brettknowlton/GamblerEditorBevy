### 🎬 _Scene 1: Bloodwheat Golem Attack_

**Setting:** Outside the census tent, in the nearby **bloodwheat fields** where refugees are moving supplies and being assigned shelters.

- Suddenly, **a golem convulses**, then **violently attacks** a group of refugees.
    
- Screams erupt. One golem **throws a man**, another **rips up part of the wheat**, its limbs twitching unnaturally.
