Humans are the relentless force of change in the world, driven by ambition, adaptability, and an insatiable hunger for progress. They lack the inherent magic of elves or the divine favor of other races, yet their sheer determination and ingenuity allow them to carve empires from nothing. Their short lifespans make them bold, eager to leave a mark before time takes them. Every great era, every cataclysm, and every golden age has humans at its heart, shaping destiny through sheer will alone.
